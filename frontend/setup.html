Generating Twitch API Credentials

To use this application, you'll need to obtain your Twitch API credentials, including a Client ID and a Client Secret. Follow these steps:

  1. Log in to Twitch Developer Console:
    • Visit the Twitch Developer Console.
    • If you don't have a Twitch account, create one. Otherwise, log in.
  2. Register Your Application:
    • Navigate to the "Applications" tab and click "+ Register Your Application".
    • Fill out the form:
      • Name: Enter a name for your app. Do not use terms such as Twitch, Stream or Channel as these are reserved.
      • OAuth Redirect URL: http://localhost
      • Category: Analytics Tool.
      • Client Type: Confidential.
  3. Get Your Credentials:
    • After registration, you'll see your Client ID in the dashboard.
    • Click "New Secret" to get your Client Secret. Keep this secret!
  4. Enter Your Credentials:
    • Return to this setup page and enter your Client ID and Client Secret in the fields provided.
    • These credentials are stored on your machine and will only be sent to Twitch.

Ensure you keep your Client Secret secure and never share it with others.
